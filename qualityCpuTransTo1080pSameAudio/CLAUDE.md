# CPU Quality Transcoding to 1080p with Audio Stream Copy

## Overview

This folder contains CPU-based quality encoding presets that transcode video to 1080p resolution while preserving the original audio stream without re-encoding.

## Use Cases

- 1080p video transcoding with original audio
- Preserve high-quality audio while optimizing video
- Avoid audio re-encoding overhead
- Efficient batch 1080p conversion
- Quality video with original audio preservation

## Encoding Characteristics

- **Resolution**: 1080p (1920x1080)
- **Video Codec**: H.265/HEVC (libx265)
- **Video Quality**: CPU-optimized quality presets
- **Audio Mode**: Stream copy (no re-encoding)
- **Bit Depth**: 10-bit video
- **Container**: MP4

## Dual Optimization

- **Video**: Full CPU-based quality optimization
- **Audio**: Direct stream copy (lossless)
- **Speed**: Faster than full re-encoding
- **Quality**: Video optimized, audio perfect

## Benefits

- **Video Quality**: High-quality 1080p encoding
- **Audio Quality**: Original audio perfectly preserved
- **Speed**: Faster than audio re-encoding
- **Compatibility**: Audio format preserved
- **Efficiency**: No audio transcoding overhead
