# CPU Quality Transcoding to 720p with Audio Stream Copy

## Overview

This folder contains CPU-based quality encoding presets that transcode video to 720p resolution while preserving the original audio stream without re-encoding.

## Use Cases

- 720p video transcoding with original audio preservation
- Mobile-friendly encoding with original audio
- Bandwidth-efficient encoding keeping audio quality
- Fast batch 720p conversion
- Streaming-ready with preserved audio

## Encoding Characteristics

- **Resolution**: 720p (1280x720)
- **Video Codec**: H.265/HEVC (libx265)
- **Video Quality**: CPU-optimized quality presets
- **Audio Mode**: Stream copy (no re-encoding)
- **Bit Depth**: 10-bit video
- **Container**: MP4
