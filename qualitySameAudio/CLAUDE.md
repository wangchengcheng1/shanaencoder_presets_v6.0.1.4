# NVIDIA GPU Quality Presets with Audio Passthrough

## Overview

This folder contains NVIDIA GPU-accelerated H.265 quality presets with audio stream passthrough. Optimized for workflows requiring GPU acceleration while preserving original audio quality.

## Use Cases

- GPU-accelerated encoding with audio preservation
- Fast quality encoding on NVIDIA systems
- Workflows requiring audio integrity
- Professional content with original audio requirements

## Encoding Characteristics

- **Codec**: H.265/HEVC (hevc_nvenc)
- **Bit Depth**: 10-bit
- **Quality Level**: CQ 23
- **Preset Speed**: hq (high quality)
- **Profile**: main10
- **Audio Codec**: copy (stream passthrough)
- **Container Format**: MP4

## Special Features

- **GPU Acceleration**: NVIDIA NVENC for fast encoding
- **Audio Preservation**: Original audio stream passed through
- **10-bit Profile**: Main10 for color depth support
- **High Quality Preset**: hq for best results

## Recommended For

- NVIDIA GPU systems requiring audio preservation
- Professional workflows with audio integrity needs
- Fast quality encoding with audio passthrough
- Streaming scenarios with audio focus

## Notes

- Requires NVIDIA GPU with HEVC NVENC support
- Much faster than CPU-based encoding
- Audio passthrough preserves original quality and format
- Excellent for fast quality encoding with audio preservation
