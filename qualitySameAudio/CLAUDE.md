# Quality Presets with Audio Stream Copy

## Overview

This folder contains quality video encoding presets with audio stream copy (passthrough). The video is re-encoded with quality optimization while the audio stream is copied without re-encoding.

## Use Cases

- Preserve original audio while optimizing video
- Efficient batch encoding with audio preservation
- Quality video with lossless audio
- Professional workflows with fixed audio
- Fast encoding avoiding audio transcoding

## Encoding Characteristics

- **Video Codec**: H.265/HEVC (libx265)
- **Video Quality**: Optimized quality presets
- **Bit Depth**: 10-bit video
- **Audio Mode**: Stream copy (no re-encoding)
- **Container**: MP4 format
- **Audio Codec**: Original codec preserved

## Dual Processing

- **Video**: Full quality optimization
- **Audio**: Direct stream copy (transparent)
- **Combined**: Best quality with efficiency
- **Result**: Optimal quality/speed balance

## Benefits

- **Video Quality**: High-quality H.265 encoding
- **Audio Quality**: Original audio perfectly preserved
- **Speed**: Faster than full re-encoding
- **Compatibility**: Audio codec maintained
- **Efficiency**: No audio processing overhead
